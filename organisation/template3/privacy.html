Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. At Sustainable Action for Community Development and Empowerment (MYORG), we have a few fundamental principles:

  • We don’t ask you for personal information unless we truly need it.
  • We don’t share your personal information with anyone except to comply with the law or protect our rights.
  • We don’t store personal information on our servers unless required for the ongoing operation of one of our services.

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide to us through our website.
