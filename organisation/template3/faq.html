Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MYORG

How can I donate to MYORG?

You can donate directly through our "Donate Now" page using various payment methods. For larger sponsorships or bank transfers, please contact us at myorgaveland@gmail.com.

Where are you located?

Our main office and field operations are based in Ave Sub County, Ave District, Aveland.

How are my donations used?

We believe in full transparency. Donations go directly toward our thematic areas, including child protection, educational supplies, and economic empowerment workshops for women.

Can I volunteer remotely?

While most of our impact is on the ground, we do have opportunities for remote support in advocacy, digital marketing, and fundraising. Apply through our Join page!
