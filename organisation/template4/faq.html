Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with MYORG?

You can apply to volunteer by visiting our Get Involved page and filling out the application form. We review applications on a rolling basis and match volunteers based on their skills and our current program needs.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

MYORG is a registered Community-Based Organization (CBO) with the Ave District Local Government. While we are a legally recognized non-profit in Aveland, tax-deductibility depends on the laws of your country. We provide official receipts for all donations which can be used for financial records.

Where does the money go?

We pride ourselves on transparency. Approximately 85% of all donations go directly to program implementation (materials, training, field staff), while 15% covers essential administrative costs like office rent, utilities, and communication to ensure the organization runs effectively.

Can I sponsor a specific child?

Yes! Our education program allows for direct sponsorship. Sponsorship covers school fees, uniforms, and scholastic materials. You will receive regular progress reports and letters from the child you support. Contact us for more details on how to get started.

Does MYORG work with international partners?

Yes, we collaborate with various local and international partners to scale our impact. We are always open to new partnerships that align with our mission of community empowerment and sustainability.
