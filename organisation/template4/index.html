Our Thematic Areas

We focus on holistic community development through these key pillars.

Education & Child Protection

Ensuring access to quality education and safety for children. We provide scholastic materials, advocate for child rights, and create safe learning environments in Ave.

Learn More

Economic Empowerment

Fostering self-reliance through skills development and livelihood programs. Our VSLA model helps women and youth build sustainable businesses and secure their financial future.

Learn More

Welfare & Psychosocial Support

Providing essential support and promoting mental well-being for vulnerable individuals. Our counselors work directly with survivors of trauma to foster healing and resilience.
