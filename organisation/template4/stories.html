Stories of Change

Real lives transformed through your support.

Amina's Journey to Independence

Amina, a mother of three in Ave Sub County, struggled to provide for her family. Through MYORG's Economic Empowerment program, she joined a savings group and received training in small business management.

"Before MYORG, I didn't know how I would pay for my children's school fees. Now, I run a successful market stall and my children are in school. I have hope for the future."
