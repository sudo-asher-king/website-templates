About MYORG

Sustainable Action for Community Development and Empowerment

Who We Are

MYORG is a Community-Based Organization located in Ave Sub County, Ave District, Aveland. We are dedicated to creating a self-reliant and peaceful community where everyone has access to opportunities for economic growth, education, and safety.

Founded in response to the pressing socio-economic challenges in the West Nile region, MYORG has grown from a small group of concerned citizens to a robust organization partnering with local government and international NGOs. We focus on evidence-based interventions that address the root causes of poverty and vulnerability.

Our History

  • 2018: MYORG was established by a group of community leaders in Ave Sub County to address rising youth unemployment.
  • 2020: Expanded thematic areas to include Child Protection and SRHR in response to the COVID-19 pandemic impacts.
  • 2022: Officially registered as a Community-Based Organization with the Ave District Local Government.
  • 2026: Launched the "Sustainability First" initiative, focusing on climate-smart agriculture and renewable energy for rural households.

Our Vision

A self-reliant and peaceful community where everyone has access to opportunities for economic growth, education, and safety.

Our Mission

To empower communities through programs that foster economic self-reliance, promote social cohesion, ensure access to quality education, and provide comprehensive protection for all, especially women and children.

Legal & Transparency

MYORG is a legally registered Community-Based Organization (CBO) with the Ave District Local Government, Registration Number: CBO/2022/045. We adhere to the highest standards of NGO management and financial reporting.

Governance

Governed by an independent Board of Directors that provides oversight and strategic guidance.

Accountability

Annual financial audits conducted by certified external firms to ensure proper resource utilization.
