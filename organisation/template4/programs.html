Our Programs

Comprehensive solutions for community challenges.

1. Welfare and Psychosocial Support

We provide counseling, emotional support, and welfare services to vulnerable individuals to help them heal and rebuild their lives.

  • Individual and group trauma counseling
  • Support groups for survivors of SGBV
  • Referral pathways for medical and legal aid

2. Economic Empowerment

Through micro-finance initiatives, savings groups, and business training, we enable families to achieve financial stability.

  • Village Savings and Loan Associations (VSLA)
  • Entrepreneurship and financial literacy training
  • Seed capital grants for women-led startups

3. Education and Child Protection

We advocate for child rights, support school enrollment, and create safe environments for children to learn and grow.

  • Provision of scholastic materials (books, pens, uniforms)
  • Child Rights Clubs in local schools
  • Monitoring and reporting of child abuse cases

4. Livelihood and Skills Development

Vocational training programs that equip youth and women with practical skills for employment and entrepreneurship.

  • Tailoring and garment cutting courses
  • Modern agribusiness and value addition techniques
  • Carpentry and masonry for out-of-school youth

5. Advocacy and Community Engagement

We mobilize the community to speak up on issues affecting them and participate in decision-making processes.

  • Community Barazas for local accountability
  • Radio talk shows on human rights and governance
  • Capacity building for community leaders

6. SRHR & SGBV

Promoting Sexual and Reproductive Health Rights and fighting against Sexual and Gender-Based Violence.

  • Menstrual health hygiene management for schools
  • SGBV awareness campaigns and survivor support
  • Access to youth-friendly reproductive health services
